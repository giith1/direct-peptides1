What Is KPV?

KPV consists of three amino acids: lysine (K), proline (P), and valine (V). This tiny molecule reduces inflammation and supports tissue repair, making it a promising candidate for conditions like inflammatory bowel disease (IBD) and other chronic inflammation-related disorders. Mechanism of Action

KPV works by targeting key inflammatory pathways. It inhibits pro-inflammatory cytokines molecules that drive inflammation through interaction with the melanocortin-1 receptor (MC1R). By binding to MC1R, KPV dampens inflammatory signals while protecting tissue, reducing damage without suppressing the immune system entirely. This makes it particularly valuable for chronic inflammatory conditions like IBD.
